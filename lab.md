### Pervasive Perception and Computing Systems (PPCS) Lab @ National Taipei University of Technology

The PPCS Lab is currently being established at National Taipei University of Technology. We welcome motivated M.S. students and undergraduate researchers interested in pervasive intelligence, edge AI, multimodal intelligent sensing, AIoT, and cyber-physical systems.

### Research Directions
- Pervasive intelligence
- Edge AI
- Multimodal intelligent sensing
- AIoT
- Cyber-physical systems
